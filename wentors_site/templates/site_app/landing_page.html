Key facts about the We-Discover Program

  • It is Wentors' largest mentorship cohort for women in technology
  • It is for final-year students in college/university, recent graduates, entry-level women in tech, or women transitioning into the industry.
  • It is a 6-week program for global participants
  • It features a minimum of 2 soft skill trainings by industry experts
  • Mentors in the program are primarily volunteers from top organizations around the world.
  • Mentorship offerings are offered via partnership and collaborations.
  • Categories: We-Learn , We-Wentor, We-Work
apply now

We-Learn

Online learning opportunity for early career and non-experienced applicants in Cloud Engineering, Product Management and Data Science

We-Wentor

A 6 weeks mentorship program with soft skills training sessions in areas like e.g. Imposter Syndrome, Personnal Branding & Reputation Management, Presentation Skills

We-Work

Internship opportunities for qualified wentees who have successfully completed the IWD cohort. In partnership with organizations like Flapmax AI, Outreachy etc.
